The Best Sonic Blasts You Should Try in 2025

When it comes to Sonic Blasts, 2025 is shaping up to be an exciting year with new options and classic favorites. Here are the top 5 Sonic Blasts that you should try this year:

Oreo Sonic Blast

The Oreo Sonic Blast is a timeless classic. With generous chunks of Oreo cookies mixed into smooth vanilla ice cream, it’s the perfect treat for any Oreo fan. The combination of crunchy cookie and creamy ice cream is pure bliss, and it’s always a popular choice.

Peanut Butter Cup Sonic Blast

Peanut butter and chocolate lovers will go wild for the Peanut Butter Cup Sonic Blast. This treat features chunks of peanut butter cups mixed into a rich chocolate ice cream base. The blend of flavors is nothing short of magical, and it’s a must-try for anyone craving something indulgent.

Caramel Brownie Sonic Blast

For those who can’t get enough of caramel, the Caramel Brownie Sonic Blast is a perfect choice. The rich, buttery caramel pairs beautifully with chewy brownie chunks and smooth vanilla ice cream. It's the perfect balance of sweetness and richness.

Snickers Sonic Blast

If you love a bit of everything in your dessert, the Snickers Sonic Blast is a dream come true. Featuring pieces of Snickers bars—chocolate, peanuts, caramel, and nougat—this blast offers a complex and satisfying flavor profile. Plus, it has the added bonus of being filling and super satisfying.

Cotton Candy Sonic Blast

For something fun and colorful, the Cotton Candy Sonic Blast is the way to go. The blue and pink swirls of cotton candy flavor mixed with vanilla ice cream make it a light, fun option for kids (or adults who are kids at heart). The sweet, sugary taste is pure nostalgia.
